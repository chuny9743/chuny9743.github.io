## About

I am currently a **Research Assistant at [ENCODE Lab](https://westlake-encode-lab.github.io/), [Westlake University](https://en.westlake.edu.cn/)**, supervised by **[Prof. Huan Wang](https://huanwang.tech/)**.  
My research focuses on **Efficient AI** and **Time Series Analysis**, with interests in computer vision and intelligent systems.

Previously, I worked as a **Research Assistant at the [Institute of Advanced Studies in Natural Sciences](https://zkgyy.bnu.edu.cn/), [Beijing Normal University](https://www.bnu.edu.cn/)**, supervised by **[Prof. Weijia Jia](https://zkgyy.bnu.edu.cn/yjjg/rgznywlwlyjywy/rcdwdh/f53f9ed26e914cae904594b4a7d3d67d.htm)**, where I conducted research on **3D Scene Reconstruction** and **autonomous inspection based on Unitree GO2 quadruped robots**.
