## About Me

I am currently a **Research Assistant at ENCODE Lab, Westlake University**, supervised by **Prof. Huan Wang**.

Previously, I worked as a **Research Assistant at the Institute of Advanced Studies in Natural Sciences, Beijing Normal University**, supervised by **Prof. Weijia Jia**, where I conducted research on **3D Scene Reconstruction and autonomous inspection for Unitree GO2 quadruped robots**.
